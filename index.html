MOTIVATION

Our goal was to come up with a model for generating playlists that will maximize followers for the most common user-specified genres or search filters. We present to Spotify insight into the features and/or song qualities that users care the most about.

 

In addition to variables obtained from Spotify’s API, we will investigate other potential predictors, potentially using the ISRC number provided in the data to identify songs on alternate databases such as Wikipedia, Billboard, and others. These databases will provide information about artist and song popularities, as well as additional song characteristics, including tempo and loudness.


Our final deliverable to Spotify is a set of playlists that are most likely to be highly successful. These playlists are based around artist genre (as we did not have data on song genre).
